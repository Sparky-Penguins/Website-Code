About Us

Established in 2020, Sparky Penguins has quickly become one of Michigan’s leading electrical companies. Our journey began with the encouragement of friends and family, which inspired me to attend Jackson College and complete the Electrician Program, earning an Associate Degree in Electrical Technology. After years of hard work and dedication, I founded Sparky Penguins with a team of three skilled professionals. We proudly serve a diverse range of projects—from residential and commercial to industrial applications. As technology has advanced, so have we. Today, our expertise spans high-voltage systems, including transformers and single- and three-phase installations, as well as low-voltage automation involving microcontrollers and programmable logic controllers. At Sparky Penguins, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and innovative solutions for all your electrical needs.
